About Me

It's time for something new. My work history began in finance and transitioned to IT several years later. To be honest, never in a million years would I have thought I'd end up in tech, but looking back, there were signs.
As a kid, I remember taking gadgets apart that were lying around the house because I was curious to see how things worked. Even though there’s nothing particularly interesting about the innards of a remote control, I loved seeing what’s on the inside.
Later on, I was bitten by the creative bug and developed a taste to express my creative side. I wrote poems and short stories in my spare time. As I write this, now I’m wondering how I got into finance.
After working in finance for a few years, it got boring and mundane; I hated it! I saw an advertisement on the train for an IT program and gave it a try. I loved it in the beginning and now I’m ready for another transition. I recently took a bootcamp course and began learning front and back-end technologies. It wasn’t easy. I learned in 6 months what some developers learn over 20 years, but I stuck with the program as it got more and more challenging because I started to love it! I see the potential for something great. I’m eager to work for an employer that will cultivate my talents and create a space where I can continue learning to improve my craft.
